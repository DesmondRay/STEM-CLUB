EVERYTHING YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT US

We are a group of students from both stem and non-stem majors who all share a passion for science, engineering, technology and math. Our club meetings are focused around teaching our members the skills needed to succeed in the tech industry. We meet Sundays and Wednesdays in the S building - room 414 at 2pm.

FIVE PROJECT BASED COURSES PER SEMESTER

At HCCC STEM CLUB, we believe that that best way to learn is by doing. Every semester we have five courses that provide members with hands on exprience in different technologies and a working project to add to their portfolio.
